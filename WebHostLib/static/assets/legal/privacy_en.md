# Privacy Policy
_Last updated: February 18, 2026_

## 1. Scope
This Policy explains how SekaiLink handles personal data on website, account, and multiplayer services.

## 2. Data We Process
- Account data: email, display name, locale, linked social account identifiers.
- Session data: room membership, slot name, status, logs required for service operation.
- Security data: IP, user agent, authentication metadata, moderation records.
- Support data: messages sent to support channels.

## 3. Why We Process Data
- Account authentication and recovery.
- Multiplayer room coordination and synchronization.
- Abuse prevention, moderation, and security monitoring.
- Service maintenance and diagnostics.

## 4. Legal Basis
We process data based on legitimate interest, consent where required, and contractual necessity for service delivery.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with infrastructure providers or legal authorities when required.

## 6. Retention
We keep data only as long as necessary for operation, compliance, and security. Some security logs may be retained longer for abuse investigations.

## 7. Security
We apply access controls, network filtering, encrypted transport (HTTPS), and operational monitoring. No system is 100% risk-free.

## 8. Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your account data.

## 9. Cookies and Local Storage
The website may use session cookies/local storage for authentication and language preferences.

## 10. International Transfers
Data may be processed in hosting regions used by SekaiLink infrastructure providers.

## 11. Changes to this Policy
We may update this policy when legal or technical requirements change.

## 12. Contact
Privacy requests: admin@sekailink.com
