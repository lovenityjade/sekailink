What SekaiLink Does

One app. Everything connected.

SekaiLink is the orchestration layer on top of Archipelago: one UI for player options, lobby flow, generation, launch, social actions, and update-safe runtime handling.

Unified Setup

Configure game options, manage YAMLs, and launch from one place. Advanced YAML editing remains available when needed.

Automatic Runtime

SekaiLink patches, launches emulator + tracker, connects local clients, and keeps runtime modules in sync.

Room Coordination

Host or join lobbies, lock generation state, moderate sessions, and keep players synchronized with low interaction overhead.

How It Works

Four steps. That's it.

1

Import

Add your ROM or game files. SekaiLink validates them and stores your configuration.

2

Configure

Adjust your settings through a visual interface. Presets available. No YAML required.

3

Generate or Join

Create a new multiworld, or join an existing room with a link.

4

Launch

One click. SekaiLink patches your ROM, launches the emulator, connects to Archipelago, and opens your tracker.

Optimized for minimal interaction on desktop, handheld, and HTPC flows.

Multiplayer

Play together without the coordination headache.

Rooms let you gather players before generation. Everyone configures their own game, the host generates, and SekaiLink distributes the results automatically.

No more patch juggling, slot confusion, or tracker/connector guesswork.

  • Room links, privacy, and password flow
  • Readiness and active-YAML handling
  • Automatic patch and runtime launch
  • Moderation, reports, and social controls

For Streamers

Built with streams in mind.

Multiworld streams are fun to watch but painful to set up. SekaiLink reduces your pre-stream prep from 15 minutes of fiddling to one click.

  • Window auto-placement (best-effort, customizable)
  • Clean tracker launch alongside emulator
  • No pop-ups interrupting your stream
  • Logs accessible without digging through folders
  • Stable reconnection if something drops

Less setup stress. More time for your chat.

Stability & Debugging

When something breaks, you'll know why.

Who made this?

SekaiLink is an open-source project maintained by TheLovenityJade and a small team of randomizer enthusiasts.

This project exists because of Archipelago and the broader randomizer community. SekaiLink doesn't compete with these tools — it's a convenience layer that depends on them.

We respect the work of emulator developers, tracker maintainers, and Archipelago contributors. SekaiLink is free, open-source, and will stay that way.
