About SekaiLink

Our Mission

SekaiLink was created to make playing Archipelago multiworld sessions easier and more accessible. We saw players struggling with manual YAML collection, ROM sharing, and server setup, so we built a platform to handle all of that automatically.

Our goal is to let players focus on what matters: playing great games together.

What We Provide

YAML Management

Create, upload, and manage your Archipelago settings in one place. No more lost YAML files.

ROM Verification

Automatic SHA-1 verification ensures ROMs are compatible before generation.

Seed Generation

One-click generation with real-time progress updates. No command-line required.

Server Hosting

Dedicated servers for your multiworld sessions. No port forwarding needed.

Community Features

Friends lists, player ratings, reviews, and more to build a great community.

Custom Worlds

Support for community-developed custom Archipelago worlds.

Privacy & Data Policy

What We Collect

  • Discord Information: User ID, username, avatar (via OAuth)
  • Email: Optional, only if you provide it for notifications
  • YAMLs: Your uploaded/created Archipelago settings
  • ROMs: Temporarily stored, automatically deleted monthly
  • Lobby History: Which lobbies you've participated in
  • Chat Messages: Stored for moderation purposes
  • Ratings & Reviews: Community feedback you give/receive

What We DON'T Do

  • ❌ We never sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ We never share ROMs with other users
  • ❌ We never send marketing emails (only important notifications)
  • ❌ We don't track you outside of SekaiLink
  • ❌ We don't use invasive analytics or tracking pixels

Data Retention

  • ROMs: Deleted on the 1st of every month
  • Finished Lobbies: Archived after 90 days
  • Account Data: Deleted immediately when you delete your account
  • Chat Logs: Retained for 1 year for moderation

Open Source

SekaiLink is built on open-source software and gives back to the community. Our code is available on GitHub for transparency and contributions.

We use and contribute to:

  • Archipelago - The core multiworld randomizer
  • Flask - Python web framework
  • Socket.IO - Real-time communication
