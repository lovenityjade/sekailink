Rules & Code of Conduct

Important: By using SekaiLink, you agree to follow these rules. Violations may result in warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans.

1. General Conduct

Be Respectful

  • Treat all users with respect and courtesy
  • No harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
  • Respect others' pronouns and identities
  • Keep disagreements civil and constructive

No Toxic Behavior

  • No hate speech, slurs, or discriminatory language
  • No trolling, griefing, or intentionally ruining games
  • No excessive negativity or complaining
  • Don't rage quit - use DNF or Forfeit properly

Communication Guidelines

  • Use appropriate language - keep it family-friendly
  • No spamming in chat or lobbies
  • No NSFW content or discussions
  • English is preferred, but other languages are allowed

2. Lobby Conduct

Host Responsibilities

  • Set clear rules and expectations in the lobby description
  • Don't start generation until all players are ready
  • Don't kick players without valid reason
  • Be present and responsive during your lobby
  • Close or delete lobbies you're no longer hosting

Player Responsibilities

  • Read and follow the lobby's custom rules
  • Only mark "Ready" when you're actually ready to play
  • Don't join if you can't commit to the time requirement
  • Communicate with the host if you need to leave
  • Use DNF/Forfeit instead of rage quitting

Fair Play

  • No cheating, exploiting, or using external tools to gain unfair advantages
  • Don't share seed information that would spoil the experience
  • Don't intentionally delay other players' progression
  • If you need to release items early, ask the host first

Time Limits & Punctuality

  • Respect time limits set by the host
  • Be punctual - don't make others wait unnecessarily
  • If you're going to be late, communicate in advance
  • Don't abuse "not ready" status to hold up the lobby

3. Account & Security

Account Rules

  • One account per person - no multi-accounting
  • Don't share your account credentials
  • Don't impersonate staff, moderators, or other users
  • Keep your Discord account secure (SekaiLink uses Discord OAuth)

YAMLs & ROMs

  • Only upload YAMLs you created or have permission to use
  • Don't upload malicious or intentionally broken YAMLs
  • ROMs must be legally obtained - we don't provide ROMs
  • Don't ask for ROM downloads or share ROMs with others

Privacy & Data

  • Don't share other users' personal information without consent
  • Don't scrape or harvest user data from the site
  • Respect others' privacy settings and preferences

4. Content Guidelines

Prohibited Content

  • No illegal content or discussions of illegal activities
  • No piracy, ROM distribution, or copyright infringement
  • No malware, viruses, or malicious files
  • No advertising, self-promotion, or commercial spam
  • No NSFW, adult, or explicit content

Custom Worlds

  • Only upload custom worlds you have rights to distribute
  • Custom worlds must not contain malicious code
  • Don't upload untested or known-broken worlds
  • Respect the original developers' licenses and credits

5. Moderation & Enforcement

Warning System

Minor rule violations will result in warnings:

  • 1st Warning: Formal notice and explanation
  • 2nd Warning: Written warning on your account
  • 3rd Warning: Automatic 24-hour suspension

Suspensions

Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary suspensions:

  • Duration ranges from 24 hours to 30 days
  • You cannot join lobbies or use services while suspended
  • Suspensions are visible on your profile to other users
  • Repeated suspensions will lead to permanent ban

Permanent Bans

The following will result in immediate permanent bans:

  • Severe harassment, threats, or doxxing
  • Uploading malware or malicious code
  • Attempting to hack, exploit, or attack the service
  • Creating multiple accounts to evade bans
  • Repeated rule violations after multiple suspensions

Appeals

  • You may appeal warnings and suspensions via the Contact page
  • Include your username and reason you believe the action was incorrect
  • Appeals are reviewed by senior staff within 3-7 days
  • Permanent bans can only be appealed once

Moderator Powers

  • Moderators can kick players from lobbies
  • Moderators can close or delete problematic lobbies
  • Moderators can issue warnings, suspensions, and bans
  • Moderator decisions can be appealed to administrators
  • Disrespecting or arguing with moderators is a rule violation

6. Ratings & Reviews

Rating Guidelines

  • Rate players honestly based on their behavior
  • Don't give false ratings to manipulate someone's score
  • Don't coordinate with others to mass-downvote someone

Review Guidelines

  • Reviews must be based on actual interactions
  • Keep reviews constructive and factual
  • No personal attacks or harassment in reviews
  • Don't post fake or malicious reviews

Note: All reviews go through moderation before appearing on profiles. Inappropriate reviews will be rejected.

7. Technical Usage & API

Acceptable Use

  • Don't abuse the service with excessive requests
  • Don't attempt to DDoS or overload the servers
  • Don't use automated scripts to create spam lobbies
  • API usage must respect rate limits

Bot Policy

  • Bots and automated tools are allowed if they enhance the service
  • Bot accounts must be clearly labeled as bots
  • Bots must respect rate limits and not spam
  • Malicious bots will result in permanent IP bans

8. Reporting Violations

How to Report

If you witness rule violations:

  • Use the "Report" button in lobbies or user profiles
  • Join our Discord and message a moderator
  • Email us at sekailink@themiareproject.com

What to Include

  • Username(s) of involved parties
  • Lobby ID or link (if applicable)
  • Description of what happened
  • Screenshots or chat logs if possible

False Reports

  • Don't file false or malicious reports
  • Don't abuse the report system to harass others
  • Repeated false reports may result in warnings or bans

9. Changes to These Rules

We reserve the right to update these rules at any time. Major changes will be announced on our Discord server.

By continuing to use SekaiLink after rule changes, you agree to the updated rules. It's your responsibility to check this page periodically.

Thank You!

By following these rules, you help us maintain a positive, welcoming community for all players. Let's make SekaiLink a great place to enjoy Archipelago together!
